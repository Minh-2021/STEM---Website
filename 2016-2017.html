Overview

2016-2017 was a very exciting year for STEM Club at Bolsa Grande. many of the STEM members participated in variety of events, such as the MESA day competition, coding competitions as well as various icebreakers. At the annual Mesa Day competition, many Bolsa STEM Teams had placed in top three at Prelim as well as Regional. Not only that, several outstanding STEM members participated in Hackathon and Congressional App competition, events that challenge student ability to code and construct products that are applicable in the real world.
