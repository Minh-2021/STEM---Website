3D DESIGN PROGRAM

This program is intended to teach skills that are critical to success in futre STEM careers(Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics). Students learn how to utilize design thinking to enhance their creativity, and then use 3D printing to build their inventions. Thus, students will learn to use the design tools such as OnShape and Computer Aided Design(CAD) to create 3D models used for 3D printing.
