Overview

2018-2019 was a very eventful and exciting year for STEM at Bolsa Grande Highshcool. STEM members participated in many activities, such as the MESA day competition as well as various icebreakers. At the annual Mesa Day competition, 3 teams placed in the regional competition and 1 team made it all the way to the National competition.
