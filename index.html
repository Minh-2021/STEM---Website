STEM - A Hands-On Experience Club

STEM Club is a gathering of students that meets regularly in a learning environment to work on inquiry-based Science, Technology, Engineering and Math related activities/projects.

STEM strives to offers students an opportunity to learn coding as well as to develop critical thinking and collaboration skills - all with the goal to represent Bolsa Grande well at STEM-based competitions and preparing the students to meet the challenges in the world around them.

In the past years, STEM has placed top three in both State and National level for the MESA Engineering Design Competition.

STEM takes 1st places

Congratulation to David Chuong, Huy Banh, Minh Duong, and Khanh for taking first place overall at the Regional Level in the MESA National Engineering Design Compeition. Their achievement has truly shown the commitment and dedication in developing and building a function electronic device created to solve authentic real-world problems. We are the STEMatador!
